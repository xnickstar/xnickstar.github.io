Movember Campaign 2024

The Brief

Low awareness of Movember internally with previous campaigns achieving minimal engagement. Leadership wanted authentic participation rather than tick-box compliance.

My Role

Campaign strategy, communications planning, visual identity development, and internal messaging across all channels.

The Approach

Positioned the campaign as community-building rather than corporate obligation. Secured leadership buy-in through early stakeholder engagement. Developed content calendar mixing educational content with personal stories. Created visual identity that balanced professionalism with approachability.

Results

40% participation rate (up from 15% previous year). Positive feedback from leadership team. Raised ¬£X for men's health charities.
